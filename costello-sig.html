Here are the directions to install your new email signature in gmail:
  1. go here: https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://github.com/MrMuzik/LodestoneSignatures/blob/master/costello-sig.html
  2. copy your email signature by highlighting from the top left of your picture to the end of the wiring instructions (make sure your picture is selected)
  3. go to your Lodestone (gmail) email account and open up the settings page by clicking the 'gear' in the top right of your screen and selecting 'Settings'
  4. Scroll down to 'Signature' and then paste your signature in the window
  5. Check box below the signature to "Insert this signature before quoted text in replies and remove the "--" line that precedes it."
  6. Scroll down and click 'Save changes'
As always, let me know if you have any questions or have any additions to the signature template.
